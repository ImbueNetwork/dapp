Decentralised Venture Capital

Imbue means to inspire quality and that is what we are aiming to do.

Funding

Founders and innovators can access the funds they need immediately from their community. Our goal is to remove the gatekeeping around new projects and do away with whitelisting requirements and IDOs that plague our industry

Decentralised Autonomous Organisation

We believe DAOs are our future. Imbue wants to allow its members to vote on the projects they want to see implemented

Vested Funding

Funds are unlocked in the stages defined by milestones in the proposal. The funds are only unlocked after a vote on the milestone is passed thereby protecting funds.
