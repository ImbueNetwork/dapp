Decentralised Venture Capital

Imbue means to inspire quality and that is what we are aiming to do.

Funding

Founders and innovators can access the funds they need immediately from their community. Our goal is to remove the gatekeeping around new projects and do away with whitelisting requirements and IDOs that plague our industry

Decentralised Autonomous Organisation

We believe DAOs are our future. Imbue wants to allow its members to vote on the projects they want to see implemented

Vested Funding

Funds are unlocked in the stages defined by milestones in the proposal. The funds are only unlocked after a vote on the milestone is passed thereby protecting funds.

What we
aim to do

Innovation

Imbue enables founders and innovators to immediately gain access to the funds they need for their projects from community members that support and believe in their vision. By setting and publishing their milestones, founders and innovators can stay engaged and update community members with their progress.

Governance

We believe DAOs are our future. Imbue aims to have its community define its future, starting with allowing token holders to vote on the projects they want to see implemented.

Funding
Innovation

Each proposal is independently created and crafted by the person behind it. Project founders define their own milestones and how much they need in stages. Community funders then vote on milestone progress to unlock the funding thereby protecting their funds.

How It Works

01

Submit

Founders and innovators submit a proposal

02

Voting

The community decides on which projects are eligible to be funded

03

Approval

04

Milestones

The project team submit the milestones defined in the proposal.

05

Staged Funding

The funds defined for that milestone get unlocked in stages pending milestones approvals which are only done by the original funders.
